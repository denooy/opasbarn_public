About

Randy "Opa" Wineland

Opa's Barn is a newly constructed timber frame barn, filled with a private collection of over 2,000 antique woodworking tools nicely displayed throughout the barn. No two tools are exactly the same!

In addition you will also find various antiques for sale to decorate your home, yard, or garden.

Classes on the history and use of antique woodworking tools are being planned for the near future. Watch this website, or stop in at Opa's Barn if you are interested in future classes and events scheduled. Opa's Barn is located 1 mile west of Pella, Iowa on Highway G28.

Message Randy

Ask a question, make a comment, or plan a visit! Walk-ins are welcome any time the "OPEN" sign is displayed, which varies during the week but includes most Saturdays. For a personal guided tour or if visiting from out of town, please request a day and time to visit Monday through Saturday and Randy will get back to you. Tours are FREE and you're welcome to take photos!
