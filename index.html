About

Randy "Opa" Wineland

Opa's Barn is a newly constructed timber frame barn, filled with a world-class collection of over 2,000 antique woodworking tools nicely displayed throughout the barn.

In addition you will also find various antiques for sale to decorate your home, yard, or garden.

Classes on the history and use of antique woodworking tools are being planned for the near future. Watch this website, or stop in at Opa's Barn if you are interested in future classes and events scheduled. Opa's Barn is located 1 mile west of Pella, Iowa on Highway G28.
