Hours

Opa's Barn has no set hours during the week. Walk-ins are welcome any time the "OPEN" sign is displayed, which varies during the week but includes most Saturdays. For a personal guided tour or if visiting from out of town, please request a day and time to visit Monday through Saturday and Randy will get back to you.
